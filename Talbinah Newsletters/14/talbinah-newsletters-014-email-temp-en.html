Practical Application This Week

01

Observe Yourself Under Pressure

Ask yourself at the end of the day:
"What was my most stressful moment today and how did I behave?"
Not self-criticism.
Just awareness.

02

Ask for Honest, Safe Feedback

Once a month,
ask your team (directly or anonymously):
"What behavior of mine increases pressure on you without me noticing?"
Boldness here protects you and them.

03

Distinguish Between Firmness and Stress

Firmness = Clarity + Consistency
Stress = Haste + Reactivity
Your team notices the difference more than you imagine.
