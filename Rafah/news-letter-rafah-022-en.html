üß† Support Session: The Comfort Zone

The Comfort Zone session opened a calm discussion around a common idea:
Does comfort mean laziness?

In psychology, comfort is not the opposite of achievement.
Behavioral development research indicates that sustainable performance requires periods of stability before any major leap.

Even within Carol Dweck's concept of the growth zone,
growth does not begin by stepping outside all safety,
but through gradual movement from a place where a person feels sufficiently secure.

In other words,
a mindful pause can sometimes be a step forward,
not a sign of regression.
