Support Session: Attachment Patterns and Detaching from Unhealthy Bonds

We held a psychological support session discussing attachment patterns in relationships,
how to understand attachment, when it's healthy, and when it becomes a psychological burden requiring awareness and boundaries.

With Specialist Noura Al-Mansour, we discussed:
Difference Between Secure and Insecure Attachment
How Unhealthy Attachment Forms
Signs of Psychologically Exhausting Attachment
Practical Steps for Detachment

The session was a safe space for discussion and understanding,
focusing on awareness rather than blame,
and on choice rather than impulsiveness.
