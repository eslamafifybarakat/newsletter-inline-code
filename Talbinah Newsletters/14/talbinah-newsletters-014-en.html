Practical Application This Week

01

Observe Yourself Under Pressure

Ask yourself at the end of the day:
"What was my most stressful moment today? How did I act?"
Not self-criticism.
Just awareness.

02

Request Honest and Safe Feedback

Once a month,
ask your team (directly or anonymously):
"What is something I do that unintentionally increases pressure on you?"
Courage here protects you and them.

03

Distinguish Between Firmness and Tension

Firmness = Clarity + Consistency
Tension = Haste + Reactivity
The team distinguishes between them more than you think.
