📍 Workshop of the Week

Effective Strategies to Increase Bookings and Enhance Client Loyalty

A workshop focused on practical practices that elevate the quality of the experience and increase client retention.

🔹 Key Takeaways:

• A clear profile and defined specialty build client trust from the start

• Focusing on a specific niche helps increase bookings

• Flexible availability increases the chance of being chosen by the client

• Asking for feedback after a session supports your credibility

• Fast response times create a professional impression and boost booking likelihood

• Preparing the client before the session improves the quality of the conversation

• The first 5 minutes shape the decision: reassure the client and build psychological safety

• In digital sessions, verbal engagement enhances presence and lowers barriers

• End each session with a clear next step: suggest a date for the next session

• A clear treatment plan from the start reduces hesitation

• Following up between sessions strengthens the client’s commitment to the plan

• Reminding the client of their progress supports continuity

• Presenting packages directly and professionally encourages commitment

• The introductory session is an opportunity to build trust, not just a trial

💡 Development Corner

How to increase client commitment?

• Clarify the path from the beginning

• Connect sessions to a clear goal

• Reduce ambiguity as much as possible

🗣️ Suggested phrase:

"Let's set a clear plan for the sessions that helps us work on the matter gradually and reach a better outcome."
