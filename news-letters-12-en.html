Practical Application This Week

01

Monitor Your Language with Yourself

Phrases like:
"I'll endure a little more"
"After this week I'll rest"
"I have no choice"

If they become daily... it's a sign, not a plan.

02

Dedicate One Day Per Month Without Decisions

No signing.
No meetings.
No heavy messages.

Just: review, thinking, reorganization.

This day isn't weakness. This is leadership maintenance.

03

Ask Yourself a Frank Question

"Is what I'm doing now courage... or depletion?"

This question changes many directions.
