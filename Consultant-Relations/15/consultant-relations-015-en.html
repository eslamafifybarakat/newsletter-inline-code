💡 Development Corner

How to increase client commitment?

  • Clarify the journey from the beginning
  • Link sessions to a clear goal
  • Reduce ambiguity as much as possible
  • Suggest packages for better continuity
🗣️ Suggested phrase:
"Let’s create a clear session plan that helps us work step by step toward better results."

🔔 Effective Free Session Management

To maximize benefit from the free session:

  • Explain that the session duration is 5 minutes
  • It is guidance, not a full therapy session
  • Provide initial coping steps for the issue
  • Encourage booking a follow-up session

📌 Recommended scheduling:

  • 2 years experience or less: 2 hours weekly
  • 3+ years experience: 1 hour weekly

Can be activated via: Appointments Card ← Free Sessions
